Reiko Izuta

Personal story

  • 1935 - Daugther of Kinu and Hirosaburo, Reiko was born in Yamagata prefecture in Japan in February 14, and was called Reiko Watanabe
  • Raised in Japan, she lost her mom at a young age. Her father re married and had Reiko's siblings with his new wife. Bonus: She climbed Mount Fuji in her twenties
  • 1961 - On May 8th, Reiko got married to Gihachi Izuta, whom was living in Brazil and she had never met before. To live with her husband and start their family, she packed her things and left Japan in December 4
  • 1962 - Her ship arrived at Port of Santos - Brazil in January
  • Soon she went to the city of Registro, in the state of Sao Paulo, where she met husband, Gihachi, and started their new life
  • They had 5 beautiful kids and together they worked really hard in the field to raised them.
  • When some of their kids were teenagers and others were young adults, unfortunately, Gihachi passed away due to a severe cancer.
  • After Gihachi passed away, some of the kids helped Reiko to build her own house in the city, where she lived untill she passed away many years later.
  • Some of their kids moved to Japan, other moved to Curitiba, but one daugther, Regina, remained in the city of Registro with Reiko. All five of them got married and had children of their own.
  • For Regina's two kids, Reiko was an incredible grandmother. Loving, caring and always present.


  • 2023 - After years of fun, travels, good food, 5 kids, 11 grandchildren and 2 great-grandchildren, Reiko passed away in June 18, by the age of 88. Leaving behind much love and good memories in our hearts.
